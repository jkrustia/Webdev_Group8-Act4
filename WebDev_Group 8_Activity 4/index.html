About Us

A celebration of Filipino flavors, Namnam serves classic dishes with a modern twist, crafted from fresh, quality ingredients. From sizzling favorites to delightful desserts, every bite is a taste of home and tradition.

Savor the Best of Filipino Flavors!

CRISPY SISIG

A sizzling Filipino classic crafted with crispy pork bits, fresh onions, and a zesty touch of calamansi. Each bite offers a perfect balance of savory flavors, a hint of spice, and an irresistible crunch. Served hot and full of bold flavor, it’s the ultimate comfort dish for any occasion!

CRISPY PALABOK

A delightful twist on a Filipino favorite, Namnam’s Crispy Pancit Palabok features golden, crispy noodles topped with a rich, savory shrimp sauce. Garnished with tender shrimp, crunchy chicharrón, boiled eggs, and a zesty sprinkle of calamansi, every bite is an explosion of texture and flavor. This reimagined classic is perfect for satisfying your cravings and celebrating Filipino culinary heritage!

PUTO BUMBONG

A festive Filipino delicacy, Namnam’s Puto Bumbong is made with glutinous rice steamed to perfection in bamboo tubes. Topped with a generous layer of grated coconut, melted butter, and muscovado sugar, it delivers a delightful mix of chewy, creamy, and sweet flavors. Perfect for the holidays or any day, it’s a heartwarming treat that captures the spirit of tradition!
